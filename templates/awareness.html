Awareness Hub

Cybersecurity resilience is not built by software alone. It also depends on habits, training, and faster human decision-making.

Daily Best Practices

  • Use strong, unique passwords and rotate them when compromise is suspected.
  • Enable multi-factor authentication wherever possible.
  • Keep operating systems, browsers, and apps updated.
  • Back up important files regularly and test restoration.

Phishing Defense

  • Pause before opening urgent links or attachments.
  • Check sender addresses carefully, not just display names.
  • Report suspicious messages rather than forwarding them casually.
  • Teach users to verify requests through a second channel.

Learning Roadmap

  • Start with beginner-friendly cyber awareness modules.
  • Progress toward practical labs and simulated attack scenarios.
  • Explore certifications such as Security+ or CISSP depending on experience.
  • Follow trusted security blogs and incident reports.
