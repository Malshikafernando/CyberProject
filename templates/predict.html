Assessment Guidance

  • Use realistic values from your current environment rather than ideal targets.
  • Complete the profile section first so the report feels more professional and traceable.
  • Low training and phishing scores often increase risk even if technical controls exist.
  • Frequent backups and strong monitoring can reduce operational exposure.
  • Use the demo button during presentations when you need a quick walkthrough.
